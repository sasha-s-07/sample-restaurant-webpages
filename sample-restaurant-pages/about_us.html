About Us

We have been serving the best pizzas since 2010. We are a family run business that believes in offering our customers the best quality of pizzas. We offer a variety of pizzas to meet your taste buds. Our owner learned the art of pizza making in Italy, the birthplace of pizza. Our tomato sauce is imported from Italy and our dough is hand-tossed by our expert team of pizza-makers who are thoroughly trained by the owner himself.

Hover over the images below to learn more about our restaurant history and family.

This is Tony Rodrigo our owner and Head Chef. He brings over ten years of pizza making experience to the table. A bonus is the fact that he learned the art of pizza making in Italy! A heavy meat eater, his favorite kind of pizza is anything that involves meat. He started Pizzarium because he wanted to share his favorite pizza recipes that he had learned while in Italy with the Toronto community.

This is Johnny Rose, Assistant Chef. Him and Tony are the best pizza making duo. They both met while studying the art of pizza making in Italy. Not only are they best friends, but together in our kitchen, they make some of the best pizzas you will ever have. After all, friends who make pizza together, stay together!

We don’t compromise in authenticity and quality! Our pizzas are thrown into a wood burning oven with materials manufactured in Italy. So rest assured, your pizzas will have an authentic Italian taste because for us, that’s important. That’s what differentiates us form our competitors.
